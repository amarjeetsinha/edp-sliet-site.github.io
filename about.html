ABOUT US

MISSION
To create leaders who makes a difference in the world, Stimulate creativity and solve real world problems and to contribute to the world.

VISION
EDP aims to bring together and nurture a community of dedicated students professor and industry experts.We target to create a conducive environment for budding entrepreneurs where they can interact with investors and startup founders.We want to find tune students ideas by giving them a chance to participate in hackathons, boot-camps, business plans and competition

ACHIEVEMENTS
Without continual growth and progress, such words as improvement, achievement and success have no meaning and we believe EDP is growing and progressing through its activities and strong determination. Within short span of two years EDP achieved name and fame wherever and whichever event it participated. Among them few are

   1. Finalist at Hackniti NIT JALANDHAR
   2. Semi finalist at international financial case study competition NSU finance club Dhaka
   3. Selected for international MIT entrepreneurship innovation bootcamp
   4. Croatia MIT bootcamp, top 10 in startups competition of IIT Bombay in 2019

So success for we team EDP is not only achieving milestone but also improving lives of many during journey.
